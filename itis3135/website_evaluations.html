Website Evaluations

Bullies 2 The Rescue

Dog Shelter that specializes in all different types of bulldogs.

  • Contrast - The contrast is good everywhere except the red square in the collage in the beginning of the body.
  • Repetition - The repetition on this page is not good at all, there are random colors used in different places and different font colors where there should not be.
  • Alignment - Overall the alignmnet on this page is ok, but the margins on the right of the page are different than the margins on the left of thr page so it doen't look very cohesive.
  • Proximity - The proximity on this page is also ok. The different sections are grouped together correctly but it doesn't seem very cohesive.

Apple

Apple product website.

  • Contrast - The contrast is perfect and all text is able to be very clearly read.
  • Repetition - The same font is used throughout the page in a cohesive manner and the text colors are repeated in a good way.
  • Alignment - The alignment is perfect and the use of the entire page from left to right makes it look very visually appealing.
  • Proximity - The products are all grouped together in a perfectly cohesive manner.
