About Us

We are a group of skiers and snowboarders that hangout, have fun, and make the most of the "Ice Coast". We try to go to new mountains up north during christmas break and plan pre and post season outdoors events.

We also offer experienced riders the opportunity to compete at the college level. The UNCC Snowboard team is currently a Member of the USCSA Southeast region and we compete all across the East coast. We compete in the alpine and freestyle disciplines. We are looking for more snowboarders as well as skiers to join the team.
